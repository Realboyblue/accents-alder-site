Meet the makers

We’re a husband-and-wife finishing team in Calgary. Accents & Alder was built around the kind of experience we’d want in our own home: clear communication, thoughtful planning, and craftsmanship you can see (and feel) in the details.

For us, trim and paneling is part measurement, part artistry—clean lines, balanced spacing, and a paint-ready finish that looks intentional.

Our approach

  • Small crew, big care — it’s us on site, not a rotating team.
  • Clear scheduling and follow-through (the basics, done right).
