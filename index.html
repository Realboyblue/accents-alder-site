About

We’re a husband-and-wife team based in Calgary. Accents & Alder started after we tried hiring contractors for small finishing work—too often we didn’t get a call back, or the follow-through wasn’t there.

So we built the company we wished we could hire: reliable, detail-obsessed, and focused on the kind of work that feels like art—not “just another job.”

We specialize in trim, panelling, and finishing touches (not full renovations). If it’s a small upgrade that needs precision, it’s our favourite kind of project.

What you can expect
  • Clear communication and reliable follow-through.
  • Symmetry, spacing, and crisp lines—measured carefully.
  • Clean installs with protection and a paint-ready finish.
