Meet the makers

We’re a husband‑and‑wife finishing team in Calgary. We started Accents & Alder to give “small” finishing projects the attention they deserve—clean lines, thoughtful spacing, and the kind of follow‑through that makes a home feel complete.

Our approach
  • Layout first: we plan spacing, reveals, and transitions.
  • Respect the home: protection, tidy work zones, clean hand‑off.
  • Paint‑ready finish: caulk, fill, sand—ready for final coats.
